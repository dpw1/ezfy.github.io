How does it work?

The development process is super simple!
Here is how it looks like:

You tell us your needs

We have a chat about what you want to implement or fix on your website. Once we talk it through all tasks are added into a Google Sheets file with their respective prices and status.

We code it

A deadline is given and we start working. Once everything is finished we contact you, you double check whether everything is as you wish and if it's all good payment is fixed.

Enjoy your updated store!

That's it! On top of that we offer 7 days of free support in case any code we did break.
